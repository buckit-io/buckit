# AGPLv3 Compliance

Buckit is derived from MinIO Open Source community edition, which requires applications to consider whether their usage of Buckit/MinIO is in compliance with the GNU AGPLv3 [license](https://github.com/buckit-io/buckit/blob/master/LICENSE).

Buckit cannot make the determination as to whether your application's usage of Buckit is in compliance with the AGPLv3 license requirements. You should instead rely on your own legal counsel or licensing specialists to audit and ensure your application is in compliance with the licenses of Buckit and all other open-source projects with which your application integrates or interacts. We understand that AGPLv3 licensing is complex and nuanced. It is for that reason we strongly encourage using experts in licensing to make any such determinations around compliance instead of relying on apocryphal or anecdotal advice.
